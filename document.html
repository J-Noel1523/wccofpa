WCC of Pennsylvania Documentation


Topic - The WCC, as a group, came up with a topic that each member feels passionate about - Household Waste.


Name - The "Waste Cleanup Campaign of Pennsylvania" is the official name of the campaign. Its location is based in the state of Pennsylvania. The name was thought of by the members of the WCC.


Mission - Our mission is to help maintain household waste by using the three R's: Reduce, reuse, and recycle and a variety of other measures, such as, but not limited to: laws and help from credible officials and sources. This mission is a big task and we cannot do it alone. We welcome and ecourage everyone to take part in this initiative!
After coming up with a name, the WCC planed out this mission.


Logo - Our logo is comprised of shapes and letters. Because the topic is Household Waste, it only makes sense to include a trash bin.


Research - The researcn paper serves as a source of prrof for our mission. The paper discusses various problems with household wastes(both hazardous and non-hazardous). It also discusses problems and proposed solutions.


Action - The action is in the form of a flyer. The flyer was made after the petition.

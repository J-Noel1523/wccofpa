We Must Come Together To Combat The Waste!

Waste Cleanup Campaign /WCC/ Mission


Household Wastes are all over. They come in many different shapes and sizes. When these wastes, both hazardous and non-hazardous, enters the environment, it disrupts the environment and alters habitat life for animals. Our mission is to help maintain household waste by using the three R's: Reduce, reuse, and recycle and a variety of other measures, such as, but not limited to: laws and help from credible officials and sources. This mission is a big task and we cannot do it alone. We welcome and ecourage everyone to take part in this initiative!
